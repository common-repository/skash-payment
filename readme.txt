Have your customers pay with their sKash application, without entering any confidential information.

== Description ==
A seamless online payment method that allows your customers to scan your QR from their sKash app & instantly pay.
Features:
- Scan & Pay - Saving time for your customers
- Effortless integration
- Easy to manage
- 24/7 support

== Installation ==
Upload the sKash plugin to your website, activate it, and then enter your credentials provided by sKash support.
1, 2, 3: You're done!
